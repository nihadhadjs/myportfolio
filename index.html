Recent projects

PROJECT 01: Automatic Gesture Segmentation and Complex Movement Analysis

Technologies: Python, OpenCV, Mask R-CNN

In this project, I applied the Mask R-CNN model to perform pixel-level segmentation and recognition of human gestures in video streams. To improve robustness, I prepared the dataset through preprocessing steps such as cropping, normalization, and frame sampling. The system achieved good results compared to traditional methods like thresholding and optical flow, and it shows strong potential in sports analytics, particularly for decomposing athletic movements to study performance and prevent injuries.
